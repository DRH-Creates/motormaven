Roles & Roleplay

Motor Maven is your #1 community — small server growing fast because of players like you. Get verified, pick your role, and become part of the world.

Getting Started

  • How to Get Verified

    Create a support ticket in Discord. Then send a screenshot — press Esc → Admin — showing your name, level, and money. Being verified makes you an official part of this community and allows you to apply for PD or D.O.T.

  • Why Submit a Support Ticket

    Submit a ticket to report players breaking rules that an admin didn't catch, or if you have any questions. Do not report players publicly — always use the ticket system in Discord.

  • Role Limit

    You can hold up to 2 roles: DOT, PD, VFD/EMS, and Public Works count toward your limit. News Network, Event Planner, and Staff do not.

Available Roles

  • ✅

    Verified Member

    The foundation of the community. Submit a support ticket and send your in-game screenshot to become an official member. Verification is required to apply for PD or D.O.T.

  • 🚔

    Police Department

    Help make the community a better place by improving the town delivery percentage and keeping the roads in order. PD assists admins by watching for rule breaks and reporting them. Requires verification and application approval through MMPD.

  • 🦺

    D.O.T

    D.O.T makes the road safer for everyone. If your vehicle is flipped, needs a tow, recovery, is stalled, has a flat tire, or needs fuel — D.O.T will be there while keeping you safe from other motorists. They also help with traffic control for events and assist PD when needed. DOT does not take priority on calls — other players can still do calls and tow missions. Requires verification and approval through MMDOT.

  • 🚑

    EMS

    Respond to accidents and medical emergencies on the road. Keep the community safe. Part of the VFD/EMS department.

  • 🚒

    Fire

    Handle vehicle fires, hazardous situations, and emergency rescues across MotorTown. Part of the VFD/EMS department.

  • 🚗

    General Public

    Just here to drive? Jump in as a civilian and enjoy everything MotorTown has to offer — no application needed.

  • 🚩

    Pilot Car

    Lead and escort oversize loads — flagging traffic, clearing the path, and keeping every move safe. Must use yellow flashing lights and be clearly marked as an escort vehicle.

Other Roles

Motor Maven also offers additional community roles beyond the main roleplay positions.

  • Staff

    We're still looking for staff members. If you're interested in helping run and grow Motor Maven, apply through a support ticket in Discord.

  • News Network

    Cover events, report on community happenings, and keep members informed. Does not count toward your 2-role limit.

  • Event Planner

    Organize and run community events in-game. Does not count toward your 2-role limit.

  • And More

    Additional roles are available via the reaction roles channel in Discord. Check it out after joining for the full list.
