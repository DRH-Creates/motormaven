Server Rules

Rules of the Road

By joining the Discord and game server, you automatically agree to these rules. Failure to read them is not an excuse and will not exempt you from consequences.

🧠 General Courtesy

  • Be mindful of other players when doing jobs.
  • Do not take everything from a pickup location — leave some for others unless you plan to refill it.
  • Be considerate of players doing the same job as you.

🚘 Vehicle & Cargo Rules

  • Vehicle Limit
    • Maximum of 10 vehicles spawned at one time.
    • Includes company AI vehicles.
  • Despawn When Finished
    • Any spawned, taken, or rented vehicle must be despawned when no longer in use.
    • Example: Leaving a BMW or tow truck out while running taxi or bus routes is not allowed.
  • Camp & Fuel Vehicles
    • May remain spawned only if accessible to other players.
    • Must be despawned when you leave the area.
  • Winch Towing — You may not winch tow anything.
  • Cargo Strapping
    • All cargo must be strapped.
    • Applies to pickups, flatbeds, Lomax trailers, or any flat surface.
  • No Load Stacking (Strictly Enforced)
    • Rollbacks loaded onto Lomax trailers.
    • Cargo or vehicles hanging over trailers.
    • Tow trucks attached to transported vehicles (winch towing).
    • Goods placed in dump trucks.
    • No loose garbage bags.
    • No overloading — cargo must not go higher than the cab of your vehicle.
    • Unsafe loading — no trailer attached to a vehicle hanging off the trailer; no towing any trailer with anything on/in it.
    • Towing jobs plus extra — don't drive a tow truck towing a recovery/owned trailer with anything in or on it.

🚓 Law Enforcement & Exploits

  • No Cheating or Exploiting — Absolutely no modding, cheating, or exploiting.
  • Police Evasion
    • Running on foot or teleporting while being chased by police is not allowed.
    • May result in a permanent ban.
    • You may not repeatedly run from police.
  • Police & DOT Vehicles
    • Police vehicles are restricted to department members only.
    • MMPD & MMDOT require verification and application approval.
  • Emergency Lights
    • No blue flashing or circular emergency lights.
    • Only PD and Admins may use them.
    • Exception: Ambulances & fire trucks (still subject to traffic laws).
    • Blue is allowed if not flashing or circular.

🚦 Driving & World Rules

  • No Ramming — Lag happens, but repeated or intentional ramming will be punished.
  • Road Closures / Speeding
    • If an announcement is made regarding a road closure, do not go through it.
    • Slow down in cities.
  • Property Parking — Rental properties must have a parking spot within 24 hours.
  • Material Limits
    • Maximum 5 steel coils and 10 copper coils.
    • Maximum 6 H-Beams per trailer.
  • Blocking Areas (Not Allowed)
    • Delivery or pickup sites.
    • Parking spaces.
    • Roads — will result in towing.
    • Entrances.
    • Gas stations.
    • Garages.
    • Dealerships.

    ➡️ If going AFK, pull fully off the road.

💬 Conduct & Discord Rules

  • Player Conduct
    • Be respectful.
    • No bullying, harassment, racism, sexism, hate speech, politics, or NSFW content.
    • Applies to chat, liveries, decals, company names, and visuals.
    • No inappropriate or explicit names, including anatomical references or insults.
  • Server Hopping — Server hopping is not allowed.
  • Reporting Issues
    • Do not report players publicly.
    • Use the report channel in Discord.
  • Media Channels
    • No chatting in the pictures channel.
    • Threads may be created for discussion.

🚑 Emergency Services & Extended Rules

  • Emergency Vehicle Conduct
    • Must follow traffic laws.
    • Exception: Lights & sirens active while responding to a call.
    • You are not allowed to load a firetruck or ambulance on a transport trailer, including towing.
  • Chain of Command — Fire, EMS, News Network, and Public Works do not outrank PD or DOT.
  • Escort Vehicle
    • Must use yellow flashing lights.
    • Must be marked as an escort vehicle.
    • No ramming or winching other players to clear the way.
    • DOT vehicles may escort but must have the sign on the vehicle.
    • PD vehicles cannot escort as it will stop traffic — personal PD car is allowed but must have the sign on the vehicle.
  • Trailer Exception — Hopper trailers limited to 2.
  • Roles / Jobs on Server
    • You can only be part of 2 roles/jobs: DOT, PD, VFD/EMS, and Public Works.
    • Exception: News Network, Event Planner, and Staff.

⚠️ Rules are subject to change. Staff decisions are final. Repeated or severe violations may result in warnings, kicks, or bans.

✅ By remaining in the server, you agree to all rules above.
